What's the Salary?

The average salary of Cybersecurity depends on what state you are working in. Dependent on how important it is something is secure, different companies based in different states can pay varying amounts to their cybersecurity personnel, however, the average among most states is around $110,000



However, some specific positions can pay more or less than others. Analysts will usually be payed less than engineers, and pen testers are often brought in on a one-time basis, and are usually payed quite well for their testing services.
