What is a typical day like in CyberSecurity?

A typical day in a Cybersecurity career varies based on what specific job you are working.



A Cybersecurity analyst would spend most of their time studying the systems that have been designed in place, and researching and suggesting better replacements for those systems.



A Cybersecurity engineer would spend their day working at, designing, and implementing a new cybersecurity structure to be put in place at a company.



A Penetration tester would spend their day targeting and trying to break a companies cybersecurity protections in order to discover flaws in them.
